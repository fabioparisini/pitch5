The Vision

From a Store to a Stylist.

Today, our digital platform is a world-class e-commerce destination. Tomorrow, it becomes an essential partner in our clients' style journey. By integrating state-of-the-art AI, we will move beyond passive browsing to build deep, data-driven relationships, making Bottega Veneta the definitive authority on personal luxury.

Technology & Partnership Strategy

Intelligent Integration, Not In-House R&D.

Our strategy is to **leverage best-in-class, enterprise-grade AI services from Google Cloud**. This approach eliminates the immense cost, risk, and time associated with building proprietary AI models.

We offload the complex "heavy lifting" of AI infrastructure and model training to Google, allowing us to focus our resources on what we do best: **crafting a seamless and luxurious customer experience.**

Google Cloud Vision AI

For precise, instant skin tone and undertone detection.

Google Gemini Models

To interpret natural language style prompts from our clients.

Generative AI on Google Cloud

To power the "My Fitting" photorealistic video try-on feature.

Phased Integration Roadmap

A Disciplined Rollout to Maximize Impact.

Phase 1: "My Atelier" Pilot Launch (Korea) (Months 1-4)

Focus: AI Color Analysis & Style Curation on `bottegaveneta.com/ko-kr`.
**Goal:** Validate the user experience in a key market and gather initial engagement data.

Phase 2: "My Fitting" Pilot & CRM Link (Months 5-8)

Focus: Introduce GenAI Video Try-On to the Korean pilot. Integrate saved profile data with the central Kering CRM.
**Goal:** Test the "wow factor," measure the omnichannel impact, and prepare the CRM for global data.

Phase 3: Global Rollout & Optimization (Month 9+ Ongoing)

Focus: Sequentially launch "My Atelier" and "My Fitting" in EU & NA markets. Analyze global data and refine cost-management strategies.
**Goal:** Maximize global ROI and scale the benefits across the entire Kering ecosystem.

Strategic Cost Management

An Exclusive Service, Not an Open Expense.

Gated Exclusivity

"My Fitting" will be an exclusive benefit for registered clients, transforming a feature cost into a powerful **data acquisition asset**.

Event-Driven Hype

We will create marketing moments by unlocking the feature during new collection launches and special **"Styling Weeks"** to drive traffic and urgency.

The Omnichannel Bridge

In-store Client Advisors can grant customers temporary full access, **rewarding physical visits** and empowering our retail teams.

Our Defensible Advantage

The "Data Moat"

Any competitor can eventually integrate similar AI. Our true, long-term advantage is not the technology itself, but the **proprietary data ecosystem** we build with it.

By linking a customer's **visual profile** (their colors) and their **semantic preferences** (their mood) with their **transaction history** in our CRM, we create a rich, 360-degree understanding of our client that is impossible to replicate. This data will fuel the next generation of personalization.
